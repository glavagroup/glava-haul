Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything drivers usually want to know before applying. Don't see your question? Reach out and we'll get back to you.

Do I need experience?+

Experience is preferred but not required for every position. We are open to working with motivated individuals who are willing to learn, stay consistent, and take the job seriously. Drivers with towing, trailer, transport, or trucking experience are encouraged to apply.

Can I apply without a CDL?+

Yes. We offer non-CDL positions operating pickup truck and trailer combinations that remain CDL compliant under 26,000 lbs combined weight. This is a great entry point into the car hauling industry.

Do you provide training?+

Training and guidance may be available for the right individuals. We are looking for drivers who want long-term growth in the industry and are willing to learn the business properly.

How long are routes?+

Routes can vary depending on the position, schedule, and driver preference. Some drivers prefer shorter regional runs while others choose longer nationwide routes to maximize revenue potential.

Are schedules flexible?+

Yes. Flexibility is one of the biggest advantages of our operation. Driver performance and revenue potential are heavily influenced by schedule availability, route flexibility, and miles driven.

Do drivers stay out or return home?+

This depends on the driver's preferred schedule and route type. Some drivers choose to stay on the road longer to maximize earning potential, while others prefer shorter runs and more home time.

Can I transition into bigger equipment later?+

Yes. Drivers may have opportunities to transition into larger equipment and higher-capacity hauling setups as they gain experience and prove consistency within the company.

Is there future owner-operator potential?+

Yes. We are interested in building long-term relationships with motivated drivers. Future opportunities may become available for qualified drivers who want to transition toward owner-operator roles, including possible truck purchase or payment program options.

A Note On Earnings

Revenue varies heavily based on driver availability, schedule flexibility, miles driven, and consistency. The figures shown represent average gross monthly truck revenue — not guaranteed personal income.

The drivers who perform best are usually the ones willing to stay active and maximize their schedule. We're upfront about that because we'd rather work with drivers who understand the reality of the industry.
