The Process

How Our Dispatch Process Works

We keep the process simple and transparent so you always know what to expect and stay in control of your business.

1

Sign Up & Share Your Preferences

You fill out a short onboarding form or speak with our team to tell us about your home base, preferred lanes or regions, weekly mileage or revenue targets, and any specific requirements (daytime driving, weekends off, etc.).

2

We Analyze and Plan Your Ideal Lanes

Our dispatch team reviews your preferences and builds a lane strategy that fits your goals. We focus on profitable routes and realistic schedules that match your truck and your lifestyle.

3

We Start Finding and Booking Loads

Once you're set up, our 24/7 dispatch team begins securing loads for your 3–9 car hauler. We target solid, reliable freight and work to keep your schedule as full and efficient as possible.

4

You Receive Clear Load Offers

For each load, you see the rate, pickup and delivery details, vehicle count, schedule, and any special requirements. You review and accept the loads that fit your plan — no confusion, no surprises.

5

We Handle Logistics, You Focus on Driving

Our team takes care of booking, rate negotiation, communication with brokers/dealers, and sending all documentation. You focus on safe loading, on-time deliveries, and running your business.

6

Ongoing Support & Long-Term Growth

We stay in touch, adjust your lanes as needed, and work with you for the long term. Our goal is not just to keep you moving today, but to help your operation grow steadily over time.
